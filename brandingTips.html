  • Branding Tips & Resources
  • Helpful insights, tools, and tips to level up your brand identity.
  • Whether you're just starting your business or refreshing your look, good branding is more than just a logo — it’s the way people feel when they see your brand. Here are tips, resources, and tools to guide you through your creative journey.
  • Branding Basics
    • Consistency is key. Use the same fonts, colors, and tone across all platforms — it builds recognition and trust.
    • Start with strategy. Before design, know your brand values, mission, and audience.
    • Don’t skip your brand voice. Visuals are powerful, but your words should feel just as you.
  • Design Tips
    • Use white space. A clean layout helps your design feel professional and easy to digest.
    • Stick to 2–3 fonts. Too many fonts create confusion and clutter.
    • Color matters. Choose a palette that reflects your brand's personality and industry. (See: Color Psychology in Branding)
  • Recommended Tools
  • These apps and platforms can help you build your brand or work with designers more smoothly:
    • Canva – Great for creating simple visuals with your branding
    • Figma – Ideal for UI/web mockups and collaborative design
    • Adobe Illustrator / Photoshop – Industry standard for pro design
    • Coolors.co – Create or explore stunning color palettes
    • Google Fonts – Free and beautiful font options for your brand
    • Unsplash / Pexels – Free stock photos to match your brand vibe
