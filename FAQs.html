Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of design services do you offer?
  • I specialize in brand-focused design including logo design, personal ID design, business cards, tote bag designs, websites, box packaging, stamp design, paper cup designs, and flyers.
  • Do you offer pre-made designs?
  • Yes! I offer a curated selection of ready-to-use designs such as logos, business cards, and packaging templates. These are perfect for quick, professional branding and can be slightly customized upon request.
  • How can I purchase a design?
  • To purchase a pre-made design, simply message me on a Instagram or Behance. I'll walk you through the options and process from there.
  • Can I request a custom design?
  • Absolutely! Custom projects are welcome. Just send me a message via Instagram or Behance with your design needs, and I'll provide a quote and timeline.
  • How long does a custom project take?
  • Turnaround time depends on the type and complexity of the project. On average, small designs like business cards take 2–4 days, while full branding packages can take 1–2 weeks.
  • Do you provide revisions?
  • Yes, I offer 1–2 free rounds of revisions for custom projects to make sure you’re happy with the final result. Additional revisions may include a small fee.
  • What file formats will I receive?
  • You’ll receive your final designs in high-resolution files — typically PNG, JPEG, and PDF. For logos and custom work, vector files (SVG or AI) are also included.
  • Do you work with international clients?
  • Yes! I work with clients worldwide. All communication and payments are done online, making it easy no matter where you’re located.
  • How do I pay for a design?
  • Payments are made via direct arrangements on Instagram or Behance using platforms like PayPal or other secure methods. I’ll provide full payment details when you’re ready to order.
  • Do you offer refunds?
  • Due to the digital and creative nature of the work, all sales are final. However, I’ll always do my best to ensure you're satisfied with the result before final delivery.
  • What software do you use for your designs?
  • I use a range of professional design tools to create high-quality work. You can check out the full list of apps and tools I use here.
  • Didn’t find what you’re looking for?
  • No worries — I’m just a message away! Feel free to DM me on Instagram with your questions, ideas, or custom requests. I’d love to help bring your vision to life.
