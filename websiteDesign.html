Website Design (Ready-Made)

A stylish, scroll-ready website design — no need to start from scratch.

This ready-made website design is perfect for brands that want a professional online presence without the wait. Carefully crafted with clean layout, thoughtful user flow, and strong visual branding — just plug in your own content and you’re ready to launch.


What’s Included:

  • Fully designed homepage and 2–3 internal pages
  • Mobile- and desktop-friendly layout
  • Editable files (Figma, Canva, or PDF mockup – depending on format)
  • Fonts and color style guide
  • Instructions for customization or handoff to your web developer

Who It’s For:

  • Small business owners
  • Creatives or personal brands
  • Product-based shops
  • Anyone who wants a stunning, pre-designed website

Notes:

  • This is a ready-made design, not a coded website.
  • You can customize it or send it to your developer to build.
  • Slight edits can be requested after purchase (additional fees may apply).
