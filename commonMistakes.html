  • Common Mistakes in DIY Branding
  • Avoid these branding pitfalls and set your business up for success.
  • DIY branding is a great starting point, but many business owners unknowingly make small mistakes that can hurt their brand in the long run. Here are the most common issues I see — and how you can avoid them:
  • 1. Inconsistent Visuals
  • Using different logos, fonts, or colors across platforms can confuse your audience.
    Stick to one cohesive brand identity and apply it everywhere — social media, packaging, website, and print.
  • 2. Choosing Style Over Strategy
  • Pretty doesn’t always mean effective.
    Your brand visuals should be based on your values, message, and ideal audience — not just what’s trending.
  • 3. Too Many Fonts or Colors
  • Overusing design elements can make your brand look messy or unprofessional.
    Limit your branding to 2–3 fonts and a consistent color palette for a clean, strong look.
  • 4. Low-Quality Files
  • Using pixelated or incorrectly sized images (especially logos) can ruin first impressions.
    Always use high-resolution files and the correct formats for web, print, and packaging.
  • 5. Skipping a Logo Version
  • Only having one version of your logo may not work across different uses (like on dark backgrounds or small products).
    Make sure you have variations: full color, black & white, icon-only, and horizontal/vertical layouts.
  • 6. No Brand Guidelines
  • Without a simple style guide, your branding can easily become inconsistent over time.
    Even a basic brand guide helps you or your team stay aligned on visuals and tone.
  • 7. Forgetting About Print
  • Some DIY branding focuses only on digital and skips how things look in real life.
    Make sure your designs look good on business cards, flyers, boxes, and packaging too!
  • 8. Using Canva Templates Without Customization
  • Canva is a great tool — but using templates without tweaking them can make your brand look like everyone else's.
    Customize templates with your fonts, colors, and layout tweaks to make it truly yours.
