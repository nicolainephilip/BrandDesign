  • Refunds, Edits & Revisions Policy
  • Please read this carefully before placing an order.
  • Every design is created and delivered with attention to detail and care. To ensure a smooth and transparent experience, here’s my policy regarding refunds, edits, and revisions:
  • Refund Policy
  • Because of the digital nature of my products and services:
    • All sales are final.
    • Refunds are not available once the design process has started or files have been delivered.
    • For ready-made designs, refunds are not available after the file has been sent or downloaded.
    If you made a mistake in your order or have a concern, please reach out to me right away so I can help resolve it.
  • Edits (for Ready-Made Designs)
  • Some ready-made designs include light customization (e.g. changing the brand name or colors). Please refer to the product description before purchase.
    Included edits may cover:
    • Business name / text changes
    • Simple color changes (if allowed)
    • Replacing placeholder info with your real info
    Any additional or complex edits may require a small extra fee.
  • Revisions (for Custom Projects)
  • For custom branding or packaging design orders, you will receive:
    • A set number of free revisions (typically 1–3 rounds, based on the project)
    • Clear previews before final delivery
    What counts as a revision:
    • Changing colors, layout adjustments, small graphic tweaks
    What doesn’t count as a revision:
    • Major changes after approval
    • Completely new concepts
    • Requests made outside the agreed project scope
    If extra revisions are needed beyond what’s included, I’ll let you know the additional cost beforehand.
  • Questions or Concerns?
  • If you're unsure about what your design includes, or need help with an edit, feel free to DM me on Instagram and I’ll be happy to guide you.
