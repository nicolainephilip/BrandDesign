  • Design Timeline & Turnaround Times
  • Here’s what to expect once you place your order.
  • Every design is crafted with care, and I always aim to balance quality with efficiency. Below is a general overview of my typical turnaround times, depending on the type of service or product.
  • Ready-Made Designs
  • Perfect for quick branding needs!
    • Delivery Time: Within 1–3 business days after I receive your customization details (like your brand name, colors, etc.)
    • You’ll receive high-quality final files via email or a shared download link.
  • Custom Design Projects
  • For logos, full branding, packaging, or any made-from-scratch projects.
    • Service & Turnaround Time
      • Logo Design 5–7 business days (first draft)
      Personal / Brand Identity Kits 7–10 business days
      Business Cards / Flyers 3–5 business days
      Tote Bags / Paper Cups / Mugs 5–7 business days
      Box Design (Packaging) 7–14 business days
      Website Design Mockups 10–15 business days
      Stamp or Sticker Design 3–5 business days
  • Note: Final delivery time may vary depending on the number of revisions and how quickly I receive feedback.
  • Custom Design Projects
    • I’ll keep you updated throughout the process.
    • Revisions and edits may add 1–3 business days per round.
    • Fast communication = faster results!
  • Reminders
    • Turnaround time starts after I receive all needed info (text, logo, images, etc.)
    • Business days exclude weekends and holidays.
    • The timeline may be adjusted for bulk orders or large-scale projects.
