  • Order & Payment Info
  • Here’s everything you need to know before placing an order.
  • How to Place an Order
  • You can place an order for both ready-made designs and custom projects by messaging me directly through:
    • Instagram
    • Behance
    Please include:
    • The name of the design or screenshot
    • Your brand name/text to be used
    • Preferred colors (if customization is included)
    • Any special notes or questions
    I’ll respond within 24–48 hours to confirm details and availability.
  • Payment Process
  • Once your order is confirmed, I’ll send you the payment details.
    I accept:
    • GCash
    • PayPal
    Please note: Payment must be completed before I begin working on your design or sending final files.
  • After Payment
  • Once payment is confirmed:
    • For ready-made designs, your files will be sent within 1–2 business days.
    • For custom projects, I’ll begin the design process and provide updates as discussed.
    Final files are delivered via:
    • Google Drive
    • Email
    • Or directly through the platform where you ordered
  • Refunds & Edits
    • Refunds are not available once work has begun or files have been sent.
    • Minor text or color edits may be included depending on the design — please check the product details.
    • Extra revisions or customization beyond what’s included may have additional fees.
